## Guidepost: Change

I can’t go back to yesterday … because I was a different person then. –Lewis Carroll

## Thought 1

I have found that my ability to trust someone else is directly related to my self-confidence. When I am feeling strong and powerful, I eagerly embrace opportunities to be vulnerable and trusting. When I doubt myself, I start doubting those around me.

## Thought 2

Try to keep a proper perspective on everything your Owner asks you to do. Some tasks are fun, some are chores, and some are vitally important. Life can be stressful enough without adding unnecessary burdens.
