## Guidepost: Honesty
Self-reflection is the path to honesty. You must know yourself in order to be honest with yourself and you must be honest with yourself before you can be honest with others.

## Thought 1
Most of us spent years fantasizing about this lifestyle before we experienced the reality. We had an image in our mind of the “perfect Owner”. We also had an image in our mind of how we would behave as the “perfect slave” if we had the “perfect owner”. In reality, no one is perfect. You and your owner will make mistakes. You need to be willing to forgive yourself and your owner when that happens.

## Thought 2
By going to the edge and back with your Owner, the bonds of trust between you are reaffirmed and strengthened.
