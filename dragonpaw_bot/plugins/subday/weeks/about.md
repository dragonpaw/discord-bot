# Weekly Subday (Sunday) Journaling Class

## What's this all about?
For folks with the **{{enroll_role}}** role (with their Owner's permission, if Owned) we invite you to participate in our weekly "Subday" (Sunday) journaling.

There's a book, "Where I am Led" by Christina Parker. It is a set of weekly journaling prompts. It's focused on exploring the psychology and mindset of a service submissive, who either lives, or wants to live, a 24/7 D/s lifestyle, living with their Owner. We strongly encourage all subs to give it a try.

## What does it involve?
Each week consists of four super easy prompts, which you'll type up as little, or as much, as you like exploring your feelings on D/s and that week's four topics. We'll reach out to each new sub as you join the server and try to help get you started, and we look forward to helping you all in your explorations of what it means to be submissive.

## Do I have to write? I hate writing! That's punishment!

You don't need to write! Some folks prefer paper. But many prefer a notes app of some kinda like OneNote, or even just a text file on your computer or phone. Or DM your answers to your Owner. Anything is fine! Just write it on a napkin if you want, it's all good.

## Is anyone going to see my answers?
Your Owner will, if you have one. No one else does. Your Owner will check in with staff so they can track your progress for rewards. If you're not Owned, check in with a member of staff yourself and they'll ask you questions to make sure you've answered the prompts.

## I hate deadlines!
You're in luck, there isn't one. Take as little or as long as you need for each one. Each week is about 15-30 mins to complete. But if it takes 5 days, or 10, that's fine. You just can't get the following week until you've completed the prior ones.
