## Guidepost: Success

Savor your small victories and they will lead you to big ones.

## Thought 1

Accept the decisions made by your Owner with grace. If you have additional information that may change the outcome of the decision, then you should respectfully supply that information. Giving up control means agreeing to abide by decisions that you do not agree with — as well as the ones that you do. So, after you have informed your Owner of all the pertinent facts relating to the situation, be prepared to support whatever decision is made. Otherwise, the gift of trust you gave when agreeing to surrender is hollow and meaningless.
