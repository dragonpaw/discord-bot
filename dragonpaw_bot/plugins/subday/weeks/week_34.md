## Guidepost: Confidence
Success is contagious. Help others achieve their goals and you will find the confidence to achieve your own.

## Thought 1
Training a slave requires an enormous commitment of time and energy from an Owner. In order for training to be successful, the Owner and the slave must work together. By applying yourself diligently to the training regimen set for you, the burden on your Owner is reduced.

## Thought 2
Do not judge yourself as a slave by the standards set by others. When you are uncertain about a course of action, seek counsel from your Owner. If you have pleased your Owner, then you need not concern yourself with the opinions of others.
