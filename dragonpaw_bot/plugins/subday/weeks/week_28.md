## Guidepost: Inspiration

Who knows what happens when the pebble falls beneath the surface of the water? Small kindnesses may inspire great deeds.

## Thought 1

Slavery requires active participation. Even if you only consent to submit for a specified scene, you are more than just a target for someone’s flogger. The more you give, the more you will get back.

## Thought 2

Compliments should be given the same weight and consideration that you give to criticism and accepted graciously. Your gratitude and respect for the opinion of the person offering the praise will be better demonstrated through a commitment to justify the praise than by false modesty.
