## Guidepost: Dreams

Go confidently in the direction of your dreams. Live the life you have imagined. –Henry David Thoreau

## Thought 1

There is true freedom in total surrender. Expectations and fears are our only real shackles

## Thought 2

A collar is a symbol of the commitment between you and your Owner. It is a tangible reminder to both of you that the relationship is consensual. As long as you feel that collar around your neck, you know that your Owner is still willing to abide by the terms set for your relationship. Wearing your collar demonstrates to your Owner that you are still willing to surrender control.
