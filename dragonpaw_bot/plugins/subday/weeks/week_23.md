## Guidepost: Joy
Never underestimate the value of laughter. Where there is laughter, there is joy. Where there is joy, there is no room for anger.

## Thought 1
Make sure you do not place burdens on your Owner that he or she has not consented to accept. For instance, if you retain control of your finances, then be prepared to accept the responsibility for your financial success or failure.

## Thought 2
When I am allowed to see the passionate emotions of my Owner, I am thrilled by the intimacy and humbled by the trust that the intimacy demonstrates.
