## Guidepost: Sacrifice
For anything worth having, one must pay the price; and the price is always work, patience, love, self-sacrifice — no paper currency, no promises to pay, but the gold of real service. –John Burroughs

## Thought 1
How do you know if you have become truly Owned in your heart? It is different for each slave. I knew I was completely Owned when I realized that I would do everything in my power to satisfy my Owner’s needs, wants and desires. Even when my conscious mind was saying “no”, my subconscious mind was trying to figure out a way to say “yes”.

## Thought 2
