## Guidepost: Willingness

Successful service requires willingness. It must be willingly given and willingly received.

## Thought 1

Slavery is not the pursuit of pain; it is the pursuit of pleasure through surrender and service. Sometimes that pleasure comes not from the act itself but from the joy of giving and the triumph of accomplishment.

## Thought 2

When I am experiencing difficulties, usually the best way to resolve them is to shift my attention back to the simplest, basic tasks. Doing something — anything — improves my self-confidence because I feel useful and productive. With renewed confidence, the more complex problems are easier to overcome.
